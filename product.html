This lightweight yet durable rain poncho will keep you dry in bad weather. It is ideal for all outdoor activities, such as hiking, camping, climbing, traveling, etc. The rain poncho has a loose-fit design for good comfort, and is also large enough to cover your backpack. This durable rain poncho has a drawstring hood, which will fit different sizes to ensure a comfortable fit, and a snap closure on the neckline to keep rainwater away from the neck. Thanks to the snap closures on either side of the cuffs, a pair of "sleeves" can be easily formed, allowing for more mobility and convenience. Made of high-quality PVC-coated polyester, this rain poncho is waterproof and built to last.
